What is an NFT ?

NFTs (non-fungible tokens) are non-fungible tokens or basically a digital property title that cannot be defrauded but can be issued and traded entirely online, efficiently and securely. Basically, an NFT is able to guarantee the ownership of a digital asset or the authorship and licensing of a work safely in the digital universe, in the face of the scary distribution and illegal reproduction of online content.
